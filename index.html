About Us

Welcome to Richland Township Fire and EMS

At Richland Township Fire and EMS, we are dedicated to safeguarding our community through professional, compassionate, and timely emergency services. Our team of highly trained firefighters, paramedics, and emergency medical technicians work around the clock to protect lives, property, and the environment.

We serve the southwestern corner of Holmes County as an all-volunteer department, responding to fires, medical emergencies, motor vehicle crashes, technical rescues, and a wide range of community hazards — from downed power lines to carbon monoxide alarms.

Technical Rescue Team — Rescue 503

  • Confined Space Rescue
  • Structural Collapse Rescue
  • Wilderness Search & Rescue

Our Apparatus

Built to respond.

Our fleet supports fire suppression, EMS, technical rescue, and off-road response across Richland Township and surrounding mutual-aid districts.

501

Engine 501

Class A pumper — the primary fire suppression apparatus for structural, vehicle, and commercial fires.

502

Tanker 502

Water tender supporting rural fire response where hydrants are limited — our long-haul water supply.

503

Rescue 503

Special Operations / Technical Rescue Team apparatus — rope, confined space, structural collapse, and heavy rescue.

505

Brush 505

Four-wheel-drive brush truck with on-board water and hose reel — first response for grass, field, and wildland fires.

EMS 52

Richland Twp. EMS

Advanced life support ambulance providing medical response and coordination with regional air medical transport.

ATV

RTFD Polaris

Off-road unit for wilderness rescue and brush fires in areas our full-size apparatus can't reach.
